Forest SaVR

Forest SaVR is a Virtual-Reality application to raise awareness of deforestation . It is developed as a multi-semester project for the Bachelor in Computer Science of the University of Luxembourg. This project is made as part of the VR/AR LAB with Jean Botev. Forest SaVR is developed in unity . Forest SaVR received the best demo/poster award at the 2020 GI VR/AR Workshop in Trier, Germany. - Version showed was made in 2019, newer version in development and it will manke everything better!

Proof Guide

Proof Guide is a website with interactive lessons on mathematical proofs and their applications. The objective was to make an interactive, enjoyable, and understandable learning experience. It was developed as a semester project for the Bachelor in Computer Science of the University of Luxembourg. - Made in 2019
